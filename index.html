About

I’m an artist working with leather as both canvas and medium. My background in drawing and tattoo-style linework shapes everything I make. I’m inspired by the idea that objects should grow more meaningful the longer they’re used. I care deeply about making things that feel honest, tactile, and personal. I take on custom work and love collaborating with people who value the process as much as the final piece.

  • Hand bags & Wallets
  • Jackets & Shoes
  • Desk mats & chairs
